Unipick: A Utility for Selecting an Institution for an Undergraduate Career

Created by: Ophir Gal & I'Shea Boyd

Quick How-To Guide

  1. Filter the schools using the widgets under the "Filters" section.
  2. Select schools of interest by directly clicking on the corresponding graduation cap symbol on the map.
  3. You can zoom by scrolling or pan by clicking and draging.
  4. If you wish to deselect a certain state or school, click the "x" button next to it inside the list.
  5. When you are ready to compare the schools you have selected, click "Compare Schools!"
  6. When displayed the two bar charts use them to compare enrollement and cost of attendance for the schools you selected.
